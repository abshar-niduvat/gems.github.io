Nurturing Excellence in Education

Welcome to GEMS, a leading educational institution nestled in the serene and picturesque location of Ettayar, just two kilometers away from Mayyil along the Mayyil Chalode road. As the pioneer institution of the Gracious Educational Charitable Trust, Mayyil, we are fully committed to nurturing the educational progress of our students.

At GEMS, we take pride in providing exceptional facilities that adhere to CBSE regulations. Our state-of-the-art amenities include smart classrooms, a well-stocked library, a spacious playground, and indoor badminton courts. Moreover, we prioritize computer allied studies, integrating technology seamlessly into our curriculum to enhance digital literacy.

Join us at GEMS, where the tranquility of our location harmonizes with our commitment to quality education and holistic development.

Classes for Your Kids

Academics & Transportation

At GEMS School, academic excellence is at the heart of everything we do. Our dedicated teachers and comprehensive curriculum ensure that our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for success. We have embraced the power of technology in education and have transitioned from traditional blackboard teaching to interactive board teaching. Through the use of innovative and interactive software, we engage students in the learning process, sparking their curiosity and fostering a love for learning. We take pride in the achievements of our students in CBSE examinations, consistently securing 100% results and setting a benchmark for excellence.

To make commuting easier for our students, GEMS School offers an optional school bus facility. Our buses are equipped with all necessary safety measures and are accompanied by female attendants. This facility is available within a radius of 10 km, ensuring a convenient and secure mode of transportation for our students to and from school.

Clubs & Activities

At GEMS School, we believe that education goes beyond textbooks. We offer a variety of active clubs that provide students with opportunities to explore their interests and develop essential life skills. Our Eco Club instills environmental consciousness and challenges students to think critically about their role in preserving the natural world. The Maths Club makes learning mathematics enjoyable and interactive, while the Science Club promotes scientific thinking and encourages students to explore new ideas. The Social Science Club fosters a sense of responsibility and understanding of historical culture, and the Health Club focuses on developing physical, mental, and social well-being. Additionally, we offer Yoga and Karate programs to promote mindfulness, physical fitness, and self-defense skills.

Co-curricular & PTA Activities

At GEMS School, we believe that co-curricular activities are an integral part of a well-rounded education. Our students have the opportunity to participate in a wide range of activities such as drawing, art and craft, recitation, annual day celebrations, sports events, dance competitions, quizzes, creative writing, fancy dress, storytelling, festivals and celebrations, and PowerPoint presentations. These activities help students develop their creativity, leadership skills, teamwork, and self-expression.

Our Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) plays a crucial role in fostering communication and collaboration between home and school. Regular PTA meetings are held, providing a platform for parents and teachers to discuss student progress, school initiatives, and address any concerns. The PTA also organizes various activities and events that enhance the school's sense of community and support the overall development of our students.
